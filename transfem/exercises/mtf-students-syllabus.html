MTF Students Syllabus

1

Discovering New Skills

As you gain new skills and learn new information, you will learn to:

  • Manage your breath freely and without tension.
  • Manipulate your resonance chambers to create tonally consistent, forward placed sound.
  • Manipulate your vocal fold mass to create a thin, healthy sound.
  • Implement elements of feminine prosody.
2

Putting It Together

Combining your new skills, you will learn to:

  • Utilize techniques to reset and recover your feminine voice.
  • Explore non-speech sounds.
  • Adjust the balance of tonal elements in your voice in real-time.
  • Utilize your feminine voice in a variety of settings in lesson, in practice, and in application.
3

Modifying Your Sound

As you begin to embody your voice, you will learn to:

  • Use varying textures in your voice.
  • Communicate holistically with vocal and physical expression.
  • Explore different emotions and how they impact your feminine voice.
  • Delve into your unique sound!
